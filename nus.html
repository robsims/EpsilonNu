Epsilon Nus


Epsilon Nus is our chapter's official newsletter, which is published each semester. Inside Epsilon Nus you will find insightful articles written by Brothers of our chapter, both Active and Alumni about a broad range of topics. We take great pride in putting together this newsletter, and we send copies to all of our alumni as well as other chapters in the Northeast District and to our national headquarters in Stillwater, Oklahoma. While we are all a nation of Brothers united as one, each chapter has its own unique experiences and thoughts. This is our voice.
